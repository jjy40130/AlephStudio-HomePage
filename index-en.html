Game Introducion

The confrontation between the protagonist and some characters who pursue only freedom and novelty, and the characters of the worldview who live in a set framework and their respective duties under order in the dark atmosphere of society as a whole. In a world where nothing is set, the player freely explores and creates a story.

Achi Village, the hometown of the protagonist and a symbol of woodworking

I think it's a face I've never seen before. Have you moved in? Hmm, as you can see, Achi Village mainly does woodworking. Don't do useless things, and learn woodworking or something.

Comfortable and warm visuals, a dark story that doesn't match it

2022 development plan


  • Game
    Plan
    Development

    Feb. 2022

    Start level of game planning and development

    After planning and envisioning the overall system building story and game play method, this is the actual game development start level. Levels include player actions, ground and air monster implementation, dialogue system implementation, inventory and item implementation, multi-language support, 5 map implementation, and more.

  • Overall
    System
    Construction

    Jun. 2022

    Overall system completion level

    This is level where the overall framework of the game is completed. This is the level where the implementation of 8 maps, including player skills, various events, map system, inventory system, and more.

    Crowd
    Funding

    Jul. 2022

    Crowdfunding and game testing level

    We conduct crowdfunding and test play on 텀블벅 and Patreon for purchasing, testing, and localizing assets necessary for game development.
